Quotes and My Interpretation

"The only way to do great work is to love what you do." - Steve Jobs
Interpretation: Steve Jobs, the co-founder of Apple Inc., emphasized the importance of passion in achieving greatness. This quote suggests that genuine love for one's work is a driving force behind remarkable accomplishments. Many other quotes a similar idea of the importatance of passion. It allows you to "embrace the struggle" more, and have fun regardless of the outcome. This mentality is healthy.
"The only thing we have to fear is fear itself." - Franklin D. Roosevelt
Interpretation: This quote is from Franklin D. Roosevelt during the Great Depression. The situation was very grim. This quote motivated many people. It conveys the idea that fear, more than any external challenge, is the primary obstacle to progress and success.
